1-on-1 COVID Tech Help

A Place for Nonprofit and Religious Organizations to Get Volunteer Help From Technology Workers.

In these unprecedented Coronavirus times a lot of us are finding ourselves out of our element, trying just to tread water. We need help. And at the same time, we want to help others.

Community/Religious leaders are being forced, often for the first time, to digitize their worlds. Technology workers are being forced to redefine what community means.

So we’re bringing these two groups of people together to help each other. Specifically, by having technology workers volunteer to lend a hand to folks who could really benefit from their tech expertise.

Everybody wins because, as it turns out, helping others is the quickest path to helping ourselves.
